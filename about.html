Slacker Tape Group was started in 2022 by Bruno Grossi.

Upon listening to Hamladie & Friends, Bruno conceptualized the idea of a compilation of music his friends from Edmonton had made over the past few years. This would go onto become Taste of Edmonton #1. Slacker Tape Group would go on to release new projects as well as re-issuing older material onto cassette.

The Slacker Tape Group website is a hub for Edmonton artists and hosts music, artwork, articles, and other downloads as well.
