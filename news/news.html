RISLING! - KLEINKRIEG streaming release + Taste of Edmonton #2 Deadline extension

10 January 2025

Good Morning folks,

First things first, RISLING's new album "KLEINKRIEG" has just released on streaming this morning. It's a fantastic album that will eventually be getting a physical release in the future here on Slacker Tapes. For now, go give it a listen on streaming!

You'll probably have noticed Slacker hasn't done much these last days few days here. Truth is that life is BUSY and this can only get so much attention, but I really do love doing all the tape label stuff.

Though as a result the schedules have been shuffled around quite a bit and Taste of Edmonton #2 will be getting done later than anticipated.

The good news is that I will be extending the deadline for submissions until March 1st 2025!!

I've really enjoyed the submissions I've gotten so far, the local music scene here is so cool and good, I'll be reaching out to each submission soon to let you know!

If you are at all considering on submitting, take this as your call to action to send in your music!! All guidelines remain the same and are on the website still.

That's it for now, thanks for the patience everyone.

-bzg
