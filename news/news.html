August Updates

Aug 2, 2024

Hello folks.

Maintaining a website is actually a lot harder than I ever thought, but I am back to focusing on it.

There are many announcements for now that I hope to make a more consistent and regular occurrence so I do not dump a billion things down all at once.

First, RISLING! & Low Earth Orbit’s Sketches of Bruderheim is now out! It’s been on streaming for a bit and tapes are available to be purchased. They’ve already been going so if you want one make an order!

Second, Sleeve of Skin left the basement and performed at the aviary. Thanks for coming to that show if you did. If you didn’t, now you need to book us.

Third, many blank tapes have happened upon the Slacker House doorstep so expect new recordings to be available very soon on cassettes. We might also have some CDs, but I didn’t tell you that. Expect the Sleeve of Skin live performance to be released soon.

Fourth, I’m working on expanding the website a bit more so if you are an Edmonton-based artist/writer/photographer/whatever, use the contact page to get your shit published online!

That’s it for now, thanks everybody for holding in.

-bzg
