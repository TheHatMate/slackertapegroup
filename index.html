Holy Family at the sixth Noisecafe

Mar 4, 2024

On March 26th a band called Holy Family will play Noisecafe at the Aviary. This is a group formed of the artists behind Low Earth Orbit, Electric Bench Hoop and ProV1der (aka Bruno, Eli & Jackson). We are looking forward to playing our set and especially excited about the other artists too. Make sure to come out for it. Weather should be good so we accept no excuses.
