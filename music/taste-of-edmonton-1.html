Taste of Edmonton #1

V/A

Oct 4, 2022

Experimental, Folk, Bedroom Pop, Noise Rock, Ambient, Drone, Twee, Indie Rock, Electronic

Edmonton. The frozen Canadian hellscape has long been a locality for a burgeoning underground music scene. The songs catalogued in this compilation put on display some of the flavours presented by the musicians originating from the Edmonton area.
