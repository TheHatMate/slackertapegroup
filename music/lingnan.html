Lingnan

Low Earth Orbit

March 3, 2023

Harsh Noise

Inspired by a delicious meal at one of Edmonton's staple restaurants, Low Earth Orbit's tape Lingnan is an exploration of what makes frozen winters beautiful. Indulging in dense landscapes of noise and wavering wintry imagery, Lingnan conveys a sense of life buried beneath who knows how many tons of snow that surround our little town.
