Hilarious Heathenry

Electric Bench Hoop

Feb 11, 2024

Experimental Electronic

Electric Bench Hoop is the project of Eli Heatherington. Edmonton-based musician whose work defies any one specific description. The songs can be wild, poetic, sombre, driving or intense; sometimes all at the same time. Drawing inspiration from classical, folk, free music and jazz, the directions that seem all over, converge into an incredibly focused and directed piece of work.

Originally released on soundcloud April 27, 2023

To order a tape, consult the bandcamp page or contact me at any one of the links :)
