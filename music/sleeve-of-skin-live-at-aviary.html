Live @ The Aviary

Sleeve of Skin

September 4, 2024

Noisecore, Grindcore

Sleeve of Skin is a grindcore band from Edmonton, AB. Eschewing typical songwriting formats opting for a noisier, care-free approach to creating songs. Adapting performance techniques to lean into improvised music.

They are a duo of Bruno and Jackson. They made it out of the basement June 29 2024. That's when this was recorded.

Thanks to the Aviary for always being the best goddamn venue in this city. Thanks to Hollow Point for letting us play with them and No Skies & Stagnant for being great bands too.
