Sketches of Bruderheim

RISLING! & Low Earth Orbit

Apr 22, 2024

Plunderphonics, Ambient, Downtempo

Proud to present a collaborative album between RISLING! & Low Earth Orbit - Sketches of Bruderheim. An album mishmashing ambient music, hip hop production, electronic synthesis, analog machines and R&B passion. Sketches of Bruderheim can be listened to on the internet and tape when they are ready (a few days).

Order tapes & Listen: Bandcamp or E-mail
