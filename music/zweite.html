RISLING!

February 3, 2023

Plunderphonics, Experimental Electronic

Kill past gods, create them anew…

Exactly a year and five months after the release of “JUNG!”, a compilation of demos accumulated over the course of twenty-twenty one, RISLING! emerges back into the internet-music landscape with his debut album. The project is a zany, cacophonous, plunderphonic journey bridging the gaps between chiptune, pop, electronic and hip hop.

This couldn’t have been made without the support and friendship of Bruno Grossi, founder of Slacker Tape Group and artist behind Low Earth Orbit.

It is fifteen tracks containing a hundred and thirteen samples.

-RISLING!
