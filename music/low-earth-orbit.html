Low Earth Orbit

Low Earth Orbit

TBD, original rel. Aug 31 2020

Ambient, Drone

The original Low Earth Orbit EP came out on Total Trash Tapes four years ago. I'm impatient for a 5-year anniversary remastered reissue. I made this EP in about two weeks in that summer and people occasionally still talk about it. I figured that I would make a reissue so people I can give people something instead of saying they have long since disappeared.

Most of the sounds on this EP were the very first things I had ever actually put some level of effort into recording. I had developed an obsession with ambient music and synthesizers and that summer I got my hands on a Korg Monologue (honestly a pretty difficult machine to make ambient with, but I was determined). I did not know what I was doing. I did not know how to play keyboard. Yet I enjoyed the sounds that it made, especially continually layering the sounds over each other in ways.

I know what artists I was copying with each song on this project. Each song is basically a love-letter to a musician's style I had worshipped at 19. Even listening to this again years later, I'm filled with a tempered proudness of the product of the music. I manage to actually enjoy something I made. It only took four years.

This reissue I took the liberty of remastering some of the tracks somewhat. Many of them were mixed very quietly. Just some technical things to make the general listening experience less cumbersome.

-bzg
