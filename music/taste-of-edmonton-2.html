Anything & Everything

Hey everyone I'm excited to open submissions for the second edition of Taste of Edmononton. A very long overdue release. Taste of Edmonoton #1 was the first project released on the label and came out almost two years ago at this point. I've always wanted to do a second edition of this type of compilation. Last time I reached out to a bunch of people I knew in & around Edmonton to contribute because Slacker Tape Group didn't really exist back then. This time I'll be opening up to submissions and the guidelines will be a bit different compared to the structure presented on the first volume.

If you are interested in submitting to the next edition, please read the guidelines listed and send your submission!

Guidelines
  1. You must be an artist from the Edmonton area
  2. You must submit original & unreleased music.
    • If your song has been previously released as a single, album, EP, or any other collection of recording, it will not be considered.
  3. Songs must be less than four (4) minutes. Songs longer than four (4) minutes will not be considered.
  4. There are NO genre restrictions!
  5. You may submit up to three (3) songs. However, only one (1) song may be accepted for the compilation.
  6. Artists previously featured on Taste of Edmonton #1 and Slacker Tape Group are welcome to apply, though we are primarily looking to find new artists to feature on this second edition!
  7. Recording quality does not need to be excellent. We welcome lo-fi recordings as long as that is intentional.
  8. Please submit only once.
  9. Submissions close 1 March 2025. Estimated release for late Spring 2025.
How to submit
  1. Export your music to WAVE (.wav), FLAC (.flac), OGG (.ogg) or some other LOSSLESS file format. MP3's or other lossy formats will NOT be considered.
  2. Name your song file with this format: "ArtistName-SongName.wav"
  3. Create a text file with a full credits list of performers, links to your social media, and any other relevant links or information.
  4. Upload your files to some PERMANENT online file-sharing platform (Google Drive, Dropbox, etc.)
  5. Email SLACKERTAPEGROUP@GMAIL.COM with a link to your Google Drive link. Give me some information of you / your band in the email as well.
  6. Be patient! I am but a one-man operation and am taking on a lot. I will try to reach out to you as soon as I can to let you know about your submission. If I haven't gotten back to you by April 2025, feel free to reach out again to remind me, but I will make an effort to be dilligent.
  7. If your submission is accepted, please refrain from publishing the song anywhere else until AFTER Taste of Edmonton #2 is published. If you publish your accepted song before the release date, it will be removed from the tracklist. After release you can do as you please.
NOTE: This label bleeds money alone through material costs, I can't guarantee payment to anyone unless a divine miracle occurs. So until then, I'll give you a copy of the tape when it's done instead.

If all that sounds good...

Submissions are closed, thanks to all who submitted, keep an eye out for the announcement and release summer 2025!
