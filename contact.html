Disclaimer: This label does not make money. We cannot guarantee payment for any project.

Slacker Tape Group operates as a personal project where all costs of tape manufacturing are covered by me pro bono, though rarely does a project make money back (that's music business for ya). Any money made by the sales of tapes are circled back to the label to fund the next project.

Slacker Tape Group is more of a way to give artists a physical version of the music they make to listen to and share with their friends. I love making tapes and I want to give people the opportunity to have their music on a physical medium.
